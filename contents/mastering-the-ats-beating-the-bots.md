# Algorithmic Sovereignty

The modern recruitment process is no longer a human dialogue; it is a series of algorithmic filters. For the matured professional, understanding this shift is the difference between visibility and obsolescence.

## The Evolution of Screening
Early Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) were simple keyword indexers. Today, systems like Workday and Greenhouse utilize Natural Language Processing (NLP) to perform **Semantic Scoring**. 

### The Semantic Gap
Recruiters look for "The Impact," but algorithms look for "The Pattern." Closing this gap requires a technical approach to CV formatting.

## Strategic Formatting for Machine Vision
To ensure your professional story remains intact during parsing, you must adhere to high-integrity structural standards.

### Avoiding Structural Degradation
*   **Hierarchical Purity**: Use standard Heading tags that algorithms recognize as sectional breaks.
*   **Data Serialization**: Present lists in a standardized format to avoid parsing errors.
*   **Image Integrity**: Ensure all visual elements have proper Alt-text, as modern AI now scans for visual descriptions.
