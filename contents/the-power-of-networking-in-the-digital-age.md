# Relational Capital

In the digital economy, your social network is not a social tool; it is a form of **Relational Capital**. To treat it otherwise is to ignore one of the most powerful leverage points in modern career development.

## The Value Exchange Model
Networking is often misunderstood as "asking for favors." Matured networking is based on **Value Exchange**. Before asking for a connection's time, you must first establish a "Balance of Value."

### Building a Digital Authority
*   **Curation over Creation**: You don't always need to create new content. Curing and commenting on industry trends establishes your position as a thought leader.
*   **The 70/20/10 Rule**: 70% value-driven content, 20% engagement with others, and only 10% self-promotion.

> "Your network is your net worth, but only if that network is predicated on mutual professional respect and shared intellectual growth." — Porter Gale

## Leveraged Networking Strategies
Use digital platforms not for mass outreach, but for **Surgical Engagement**. Identify the key decision-makers in your target industry and engage with their intellectual output before initiating direct contact.
